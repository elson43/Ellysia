A Little Investigation

You've been on my mind a lot lately, so I couldn’t help but investigate the meaning behind your name. There’s got to be something special about it! Why do you go by Lanlan instead of Ellysia? Don’t get me wrong—Lanlan is cute, but Ellysia has that vibe straight out of fairy-tale.

Speaking of names, I found out that Lanlan means 'anggrek flower,' and I can’t even be mad about it—anggreks are stunning! Whoever came up with that name is brilliant.

I've also been trying to figure out what your favorite dolls are. I keep thinking, “Is it this Sanrio character or that one?” Honestly, I’m so confused! I just want to get it right because I know you have great taste in cute things.

Now, about the necklace I picked out for you—I was a bit nervous wondering if you might be allergic to metal since I’ve never seen you wear any jewelry. So, I finally asked, and you told me you’re allergic… to dust! I couldn’t help but laugh! Dust allergies are definitely a thing, but that caught me completely off guard.

So, no need to worry about the necklace—I’m relieved it’s not a serious metal allergy. Just keep it away from dusty places, okay? 😊
